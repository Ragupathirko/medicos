ABOUT US

Medicos Elder Care Is One Of The “Home Healthcare service Providers With A Network Of 100+Employees Across Two Cities In India. We Aim To Provide Conveniently, Compassionate, And Quality Care Possible In The Comfort Of Your Home. Our Services Include Nursing Attendant, Home Nurse, Doctor Consultation, Physiotherapy, Elder & Diabetic Care, Chemotherapy At Home, Vaccination, Diagnostic Services Patnering With Premium Hospitals.



AIM

WE AIM TO CONVENIENT,COMPASIONATE AND QUALITY CARE AT YOUR COMFORT HOME .WE ARE HERE TO ACHIVE THAT "ADDING LIFE TO YEARS"

VISION

Medicos elder is delivering quality care with compassion. We bring world-class medical care into our patients, homes and aim to make primary healthcare not only more accessible, but also more effective, affordable, and accountable to our patient’s needs.

MISSION

To be passionately involved in creating health care solutions that are convenient, accessible, and unique. To create sustainable growth in the health care sector by hiring, educating, and promoting youth and women. To provide world-class home care to our patients
